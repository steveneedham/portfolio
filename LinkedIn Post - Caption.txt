Seven years. One chapter, closing.

On June 1, 2019, I started at Spin.

Four years later, I took a photograph beneath the old Union Station arch in Columbus—mostly because the light looked good. I didn't know then that I would return to it at the end of this chapter and understand it differently.

The arch once served a railroad that no longer exists. It survived because it found another purpose.

There's something in that.

Seven years later, I'm grateful—for the team, the riders, and a city that made room for a different way of moving.

The chapter ends. The work doesn't.
