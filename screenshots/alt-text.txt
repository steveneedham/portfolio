slide-1.png — Cover
Orange scooters lined beneath the Union Station Arch at dusk in Columbus, Ohio. Title text overlaid: "Seven years. One era, closing." Eyebrow label: "A photograph, as one era closes."

slide-2.png — Field notes
Two photos from June 2019 (fleet charging in a warehouse, and scooters parked at a Pride festival) above essay text: "The era really started on June 1, 2019 — my first day at Spin. I took this beautiful photo four years into it — June 29, 2023, 11:08 PM, at McFerson Commons (Arch Park), Arena District, Columbus, Ohio — late one night, after a long day, mostly because the light looked good. I didn't know yet it would end up marking the middle of something. I definitely didn't know I'd be looking at it again years later, at the end. Not scooters. Not an arch. A city, mid-exhale — opening itself up to a different way of moving through it. The arch behind them is the last piece left of Columbus's old Union Station, saved from the wrecking ball in 1979 and moved here to keep watch over a park instead of a platform. Underneath it, a small fleet sat waiting for whoever needed it next."

slide-3.png — The throughline
Historic black-and-white photo of the Columbus Union Station facade (early 1900s) beside essay text: "Nearly 2 million rides later, that's the part I keep coming back to: that arch was built in the 1800s for a railroad that no longer exists. It didn't get to keep its original purpose. It got a second one, and it's still standing because of it. Eras end. The work doesn't have to. Movement, done right, was never really about the vehicle — it's a promise that outlasts whoever's holding it at the time."

slide-4.png — Behind the fleet
Photo of Steven in Spin gear on a scooter, checking his phone, warm orange tint. Caption text: "Somewhere in seven years, this stopped looking strange to me. A bridge, a bike lane, a fleet lined up in the fog — just Tuesday night in a city that decided to make room."

slide-5.png — The details
Close-up photo of worn shoes on a Spin scooter deck mid-ride. Caption text: "Worn shoes on a scrappy deck — the part nobody photographs, the part that was actually the job."

slide-6.png — Now (Closing)
Black-and-white photo, subtly teal-tinted, of scooters lined along the Genoa bridge with the LeVeque Tower skyline. Closing text: "So this is me, closing an era and looking at the photo that came to define it. I don't feel nostalgia. I feel grateful — for the team, the riders, and a city that kept showing up long enough to let this mean something." Signed: Steven Needham.
